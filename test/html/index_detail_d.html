Property Description

You can host a dinner in a tasteful full glass dining room. Candles are floating on the water surrounding the dining room. Have Ayana Bali standard private dining experience in your own home!

Have tea and chat with your friends next to the long swimming pool in an open air Balinese courtyard with lush greenery, even on rainy days!

Sit back and relax in the spacious living room. Quiet and windy. NS facing and its very rare fully single-loading design made air con redundant even on a hot afternoon.

The whole house is very open-concept. You will feel very relaxed and comfortable. Meanwhile, you can enjoy it in any weather and anytime. Very practical too!

Walk to Tanah Merah MRT. Future East Region line will also come up soon! No wonder big developers like FEO and Keppel had already sold nearby 99LH condo at 1500+psf. Future projects could be even higher due to higher land bids. Don't you think this amazing FH landed should have even more potential?

Located at the end of a small road. Very quiet and very exclusive.
